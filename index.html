Benefits of All-in-One (AIO)

All-in-one (AIO) aquariums are the perfect set-up for those new to the reef aquarium hobby, or those looking for compact aquarium setups.

  • The back compartment houses pump, heaters and filtration media for a clean, compact look
  • Filtration media baskets to customize filtration to your needs and media socks come with starter kits

AIO Filtration Features

  • Emergency Overflow
  • (2) - Removable 200 Micron Filter Sock
  • (2) - Adjustable 130 GPH DC Return Pump 5W/24V
  • Includes: Flexible hose, Return Elbow and Directional Flow Nozzle
